cacio e pepe

ingredients

  • kosher salt
  • 6oz pasta or spaghetti
  • 3tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1tsp fresh cracked black pepper
  • 3/4 cup finely grated grana padano or parmesan
  • 1/3 cup grated pecorino

detailing

  1. bring 3 quarts water to a boil
    • season water with salt
  2. add pasta into water
    • stirring occasionally
    • reserve 3/4 cup pasta cooking water
  3. melt 2tbsp butter in a large skillet
  4. add black pepper
    • cook swirling pan until toasted
  5. add 1/2 cup reserved pasta water to skillet
    • bring to simmer
  6. add pasta and remaining butter
    • reduce heat to low
  7. add crana padano
    • stirring and tossing with tongs until cheese meltes
  8. add pecorino and serve
